// how it works

Three moves. One living workforce.

Trees OS doesn't replace your systems. It layers on top of them and runs the agents that make every open role fill with people who are reachable, verified, and ready.

01

We build your matching language

Before anything matches, Trees OS maps how you evaluate talent — roles, skills, certifications, seniority, deal-breakers, field constraints. Your taxonomy, not a generic template. Your dashboard runs on it from day one, and it sharpens every time your team accepts or rejects a profile.

delivered in 2 weeks · the first thing we hand you
02

We keep your candidates live

Your candidates join the workforce graph once, by consent. From there Trees OS agents keep them current — availability, certifications, mobility, notice period — through the channels they already use: WhatsApp and Telegram. No more database that's stale the moment you need it.

kept current by AI agents · on the worker's own phone
03

You post a role. Qualified people appear.

The day you open a role, a Trees OS agent re-engages the right candidates by email and opens a conversation: is this for you, are you available, here's what we need to put you forward. Confirmed, interested people land on your dashboard. One click invites them, they book their own slot, your hiring manager confirms. You walk into the interview — not the search.

// what your team will ask

The questions HR, legal and ops always raise.

Who owns the candidate data?
You do. Trees OS is the infrastructure underneath; candidates own their profiles, you own the relationship and the hire. We never sell or share your pool.
Is candidate consent covered?
Double opt-in before any agent makes contact, PDPA & GDPR aligned by design. Every candidate can update or delete their data at any time.
Do we have to replace our ATS / HCM?
No. Trees OS layers on top of the systems you already run and connects into them. No rip-and-replace, no migration project.
Can the AI make a wrong hire?
It can't make a hire at all. The agents pre-filter and surface; a human approves every invitation and every decision. No autonomous matching.
Will our recruiters resist it?
It removes the part they hate — chasing availability and certs — and hands back the part they're paid for: judgement and relationships. It's a tool for them, not a replacement.
What does WhatsApp engagement involve?
Candidates opt in and engage on the channel they already use. The agent is transparent about who it is and why it's reaching out — no spoofing, no spam.
